# ClawGuard – Delete Your Account

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Open the ClawGuard app
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Account**
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Confirm your decision when prompted

## What Gets Deleted
- Your username, email, phone number and PIN
- All encrypted files and vault contents
- Your subscription history and preferences
- All account data stored on our servers

## Data Retention
All personal data is permanently deleted within **30 days** of your request.
