# ClawGuard – Delete Your Account

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Open the ClawGuard app
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Press the **red trash icon (🗑️)** button
4. A confirmation popup will appear
5. Enter your **password** and **confirm** your decision
6. Your account deletion request will be processed immediately

## What Gets Deleted
- Your username, email address, phone number and PIN
- All encrypted files and vault contents
- Your subscription history (Pro/Elite tier)
- All account data stored on our servers

## Data Retention
All personal data is permanently and irreversibly deleted within
**30 days** of your request. Subscription records may be retained
for legal and billing compliance purposes only.

## Important Notice
This action is **irreversible**. Once deleted, your encrypted data
cannot be recovered. Make sure to export or back up any important
data before proceeding.
